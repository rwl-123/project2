Dog Breeds
Dog breeds are groups of domestic dogs that have been selectively bred over time to exhibit specific physical traits, temperaments, and abilities suited to various tasks or lifestyles. Each breed is unique, with characteristics such as size, coat type, and energy levels tailored to different purposes, from companionship to working roles like herding, hunting, or guarding. While their appearances and behaviors vary, all breeds share a common bond as loyal and loving pets, making them cherished members of human families.
